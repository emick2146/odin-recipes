The Perfect Lasagna

Description

This recipe for classic lasagna features layers of rich, savory flavors, making it a hearty and comforting meal. The foundation is a robust, homemade meat sauce made with browned ground beef, onions, garlic, and a mix of crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. The sauce is seasoned with basil, oregano, and a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of spice, with optional sugar to balance the acidity of the tomatoes.

The creamy ricotta cheese mixture includes Parmesan cheese, egg, and parsley, adding richness to the dish. This mixture is layered between tender lasagna noodles and a generous amount of melted mozzarella cheese, creating a decadent and satisfying bite in every forkful.

The lasagna is assembled in alternating layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella, then baked until the cheese on top is golden and bubbly. Letting it rest before serving ensures the layers hold together, giving you a beautiful slice every time.

It's a classic comfort food perfect for family dinners or gatherings, packed with flavor and sure to please a crowd.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz / 170g) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz / 425g) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)

For the Cheese Mixture

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
