The Perfect Lasagna

Description

This recipe for classic lasagna features layers of rich, savory flavors, making it a hearty and comforting meal. The foundation is a robust, homemade meat sauce made with browned ground beef, onions, garlic, and a mix of crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. The sauce is seasoned with basil, oregano, and a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of spice, with optional sugar to balance the acidity of the tomatoes.

The creamy ricotta cheese mixture includes Parmesan cheese, egg, and parsley, adding richness to the dish. This mixture is layered between tender lasagna noodles and a generous amount of melted mozzarella cheese, creating a decadent and satisfying bite in every forkful.

The lasagna is assembled in alternating layers of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella, then baked until the cheese on top is golden and bubbly. The result is a comforting Italian dish with perfectly balanced textures—soft, cheesy layers and meaty, flavorful sauce. Letting it rest before serving ensures the layers hold together, giving you a beautiful slice every time.

It's a classic comfort food perfect for family dinners or gatherings, packed with flavor and sure to please a crowd.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz / 170g) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz / 425g) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)

For the Cheese Mixture

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef (or beef/pork mix) over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent (about 3-5 minutes)
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add the sugar, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using. Let the sauce simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth and set aside.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle with 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat this process (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce) two more times, ending with a final layer of meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil (to prevent cheese from burning).
    • Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    • Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
  5. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
